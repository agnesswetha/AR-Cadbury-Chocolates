• 5 Star is a chocolate bar produced by Cadbury's and sold in India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Brazil, South Africa, the Philippines and Egypt.

  • It is described as a "caramel and nougat" mix covered with "smooth milk chocolate" and is sold in a golden wrapper decorated with stars.

    •

  • 5 Star was launched in India in 1969.

    •

  • It was launched in Brazil the same year but under Cadbury's sister brand Lacta.
